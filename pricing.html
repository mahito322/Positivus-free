Most Frequently Asked

Questions

What is your typical response time for support tickets?

We guarantee an initial response within 1 hour for critical issues (Priority 1). Specific response times for all ticket levels are outlined in our Service Level Agreement (SLA).

Do you offer 24/7 monitoring and support?

Yes. Our system monitoring is 24/7/365. Emergency support for critical issues is also available around the clock.

How do you handle data backup and disaster recovery?

We implement automated, encrypted daily backups with off-site storage. Our disaster recovery plan includes defined Recovery Time and Point Objectives (RTO/RPO) to minimize downtime.

What security measures do you have in place to protect our data?

We use a layered approach: next-gen antivirus (EDR), managed firewalls, regular security patching, and employee security training to protect your data.

Can you help us with compliance (e.g., GDPR, HIPAA)?

Absolutely. We provide compliant infrastructure, policies, and technical controls tailored to specific regulations like GDPR, HIPAA, and others.

What is included in the onboarding process for a new client?

Onboarding includes a full network assessment, tool implementation, and documentation. A dedicated team ensures a smooth and knowledge-based transition.
